The Torus

Although not yet finished, the Sky Torus is still a magnificent sight. It is part andesite part glass and suspended over a deep ocean. It is accessible from the nether rail

The Torus was built by davemac30 and javemac30

To build it, they used a program called plotz. This enables them to know where to put the blocks (they didn't do it from scratch). They used the coordinates 128, 25, 6 if you wanted to re-create it.

The Inner Sanctum

The Inner Sanctum is made of diorite and is the 'spiritual center' of the torus. It contains the portals that are the main exit and enrance to the torus and is connected to the torus via 4 andesite spokes. These spokes go to the different quadrants owned by different people and contain different things.

The Glass Factory

Half of the Torus is made of glass therefore we needed to produce it on a mass scale. So davemac30 created a glass factory. It is in the Southern Destert (see map) because glass is smelted from sand (for all you noobs out there). The actual factory is made of two furnaces with hoppers feeding sand (or other smeltable material)and fuel (lava buckets) in, and the smelted product out into large chests.

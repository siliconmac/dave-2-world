The Torus

Dave 2 World's First 'Collosal superstructure suspended in the sky!'

Although not yet finished, the Sky Torus is still a magnificent sight. It is part andesite part glass and suspended over a deep ocean. It is accessible from the nether rail

The Torus was built by davemac30 and shmoosharoo

To build it, they used a program called plotz. This enables them to know where to put the blocks (they didn't do it from scratch). They used the coordinates 128, 25, 6 if you wanted to re-create it.
